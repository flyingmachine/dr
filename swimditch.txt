#: Swims back and forth in the Baerolt's farm ditch

match swimnorth paths: north
matchre swimsouth /paths:.*?south/i
put look
matchwait

swimnorth:
put n
match swimnorth paths: north
match swimnorth wait
matchre swimsouth /paths:.*?south/i
put look
matchwait

swimsouth:
put s
matchre swimsouth /paths:.*?south/i
match swimsouth wait
match swimnorth paths: north
put look
matchwait
